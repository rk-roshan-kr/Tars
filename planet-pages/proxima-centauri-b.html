Planet Overview

Proxima Centauri b is an exoplanet orbiting in the habitable zone of the red dwarf star Proxima Centauri, the closest star to the Sun. It is located approximately 4.24 light-years from Earth in the constellation of Centaurus. This rocky planet is one of the closest potentially habitable exoplanets to our solar system, making it a prime target for future interstellar exploration and the search for extraterrestrial life.
